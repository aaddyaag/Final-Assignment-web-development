About Silver Spoon

Our story of passion, tradition, and delicious baked goods

Our Story

Silver Spoon Bakery was founded in 1995 by master baker Bhavna Sharma, whose dream was to create a bakery that combined traditional techniques with innovative flavors.

What started as a small corner shop with just three signature items has grown into a beloved local institution, serving thousands of customers each week with our expanded menu of artisanal breads, cakes, pastries, and seasonal specialties.

Through the years, we've maintained our commitment to quality, using only the finest ingredients and refusing to take shortcuts that would compromise the integrity of our products.

1995

Silver Spoon Bakery opens its doors in downtown Anytown.

2005

Expanded to our current location with a larger kitchen and seating area.

2015

Celebrated 20 years with the launch of our signature chocolate cake that won regional awards.

Today

Continuing our tradition of excellence while embracing new techniques and flavors.

Our Philosophy

At Silver Spoon, we believe that exceptional baked goods start with exceptional ingredients. That's why we source locally whenever possible, partnering with farmers and producers who share our commitment to quality.

We're proud to use:

  • Organic flour milled within 100 miles of our bakery
  • Eggs from free-range, humanely raised chickens
  • Seasonal fruits from local orchards
  • Fair-trade chocolate and vanilla
  • Butter from grass-fed cows

We believe in traditional methods that take time but yield superior results. Many of our recipes require multiple days of preparation, allowing flavors to develop naturally and doughs to ferment properly.

Most importantly, we believe that baking is an act of love and community. Every item that leaves our kitchen is crafted with care, because we know it will become part of your special moments and everyday joys.
